1 A PSALM OF DAVID.The LORD is my shepherd; I shall not want.
2 He makes me lie down in green pastures. He leads me beside still waters. <N1>  { (1) Hebrew [beside waters of rest] }
3 He restores my soul. He leads me in paths of righteousness <N1> for his name's sake.  { (1) Or [in right paths] }
4 Even though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death,<N1> I will fear no evil, for you are with me; your rod and your staff, they comfort me.  { (1) Or [the valley of deep darkness] }
5 You prepare a table before me in the presence of my enemies; you anoint my head with oil; my cup overflows.
6 Surely<N1> goodness and mercy<N2> shall follow me all the days of my life, and I shall dwell<N3> in the house of the LORD forever. <N4>  { (1) Or [Only] (2) Or [steadfast love] (3) Or [shall return to dwell] (4)   Hebrew  [for length of days] }
