1 TO THE CHOIRMASTER: WITH STRINGED INSTRUMENTS. A MASKIL<N1> OF DAVID, WHEN THE ZIPHITES WENT AND TOLD SAUL, "IS NOT DAVID HIDING AMONG US?" O God, save me, by your name, and vindicate me by your might.  { (1) Probably a musical or liturgical term  }
2 O God, hear my prayer; give ear to the words of my mouth.
3 For strangers<N1> have risen against me; ruthless men seek my life; they do not set God before themselves. Selah  { (1) Some Hebrew manuscripts and Targum [insolent men ](compare Psalm 86:14)  }
4 Behold, God is my helper; the Lord is the upholder of my life.
5 He will return the evil to my enemies; in your faithfulness put an end to them.
6 With a freewill offering I will sacrifice to you; I will give thanks to your name, O LORD, for it is good.
7 For he has delivered me from every trouble, and my eye has looked in triumph on my enemies.
